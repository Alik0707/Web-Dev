Pancakes

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 2 people
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 1 cup
Milk 1 cup
Egg 1

Step 1: Mix flour, milk, and egg in a bowl.

Step 2: Heat a pan and pour batter.

Step 3: Fry until golden on both sides.

Cheese Omelette

  • Time: 10 minutes
  • Serves: 1 person
Ingredients Quantity
Eggs 2
Cheese 50 g
Salt To taste

Step 1: Beat eggs with salt.

Step 2: Pour into a hot pan.

Step 3: Add cheese and fold omelette.
