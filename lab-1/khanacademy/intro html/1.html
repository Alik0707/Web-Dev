The Loving Soul

A speechless tongue, yet love can speak
Seen by the eyes, felt deep inside
Love is a force so mysterious
To feel it is a sweet sorrow
Burning with fire through day and night
I lost myself, forgot my name
This feeling rose to something high
Love blazing like an endless flame
Loving eyes can’t see the pain
Loving words can shake the soul
Wandering through the world of men
Just don’t let your spirit fall
My eyes are open, my chest is full
My gaze is new, my soul alive
Is it fortune or a soul-stealer?
A fated person crossed my life
My thoughtful mind gives quiet advice
Without them, this world feels blind
My breath grows tight, my heart beats fast
I felt this feeling at last
Sweet as honey, deep and true
